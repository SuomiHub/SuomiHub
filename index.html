Lessons & Learning

Learning Finnish may feel challenging in the beginning, but every language starts with small steps. At SuomiHub, we focus on simple, practical lessons that help students use Finnish in everyday situations. Our beginner lessons introduce the most important parts of the language.

👋 Greetings

  • Hei – Hi
  • Hyvää huomenta – Good morning
  • Kiitos – Thank you
  • Anteeksi – Sorry / Excuse me

🔢 Numbers

  • 1 – yksi
  • 2 – kaksi
  • 3 – kolme
  • 4 – neljä
  • 5 – viisi

💬 Simple Sentences

  • Minun nimeni on... – My name is...
  • Missä on kirjasto? – Where is the library?
  • Tarvitsen apua – I need help

How to start learning Finnish step by step:

  • Begin with simple greetings and daily expressions.
  • Practice pronunciation by repeating words aloud.
  • Learn a few new words every day.
  • Use Finnish in real-life situations such as shops, buses, or restaurants.
  • Practice regularly and celebrate small progress.

⏱ Short practice tips:

Practice for 10–15 minutes every day. Listen to Finnish music or watch short videos. Write down new words and review them often. Learning slowly but consistently will help you build confidence and enjoy the process.

Tips & Tricks

Learning a new language becomes easier when you use the right strategies. This page shares practical tips that help students learn Finnish faster and more effectively.

🧠 Learning Strategies

• Study a few words every day instead of memorizing long lists.

• Practice speaking even if you make mistakes.

• Review vocabulary regularly to improve memory.

🛠 Helpful Tools

• Language learning apps such as Duolingo and Memrise can help build vocabulary.

• Finnish podcasts and YouTube videos can improve listening skills.

• Online flashcards can help review words quickly.

⚠️ Mistakes to Avoid

• Trying to learn everything at once.

• Being afraid to speak because of mistakes.

• Skipping practice for long periods of time.

Remember, consistency is more important than perfection.

About Us

Our Story – SuomiHub

SuomiHub was officially launched in April 2026 as a learning website made for students. The main idea was to make studying easier and less confusing, especially for people who need clear and simple materials.

Learning a new language can feel hard sometimes, but it is also exciting. SuomiHub was created for students who want to learn Finnish, feel more comfortable in Finland, and communicate confidently in daily life.

On SuomiHub, you can find step-by-step lessons, important vocabulary, helpful tips, and simple exercises. The goal is not just to learn words, but to actually understand and use Finnish in real situations.

Whether you are at school, talking to classmates, or exploring the city, SuomiHub is here to guide you and make learning Finnish easier and more enjoyable.

Before the official launch, the idea was carefully developed by focusing on real student needs. Many learners struggled with messy notes, unclear explanations, and lack of support outside class — so SuomiHub brings everything together in one clear place.

At first, the project was simple. But as it grew, organizing content became a challenge. This was solved by creating a clear structure with categories and easy-to-follow content.

After launching in April 2026, feedback from users helped improve the platform even more. Today, SuomiHub continues to grow with the mission of helping students learn together in a simple and supportive way.
